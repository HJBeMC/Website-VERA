About

Welcome to our about page.
Here you can find more information about our company.

We were once a neat student association, and that's where the name comes from: Veri Et Recti Amici (True And Sincere Friends). Today it is a fully-fledged concert stage. Many bands, which later became world famous, have once played here, such as: U2, Joy Division, Simple Minds, Nick Cave, Henry Rollins, Sonic Youth, Soundgarden, Slayer, Nirvana, Pearl Jam, Weezer, The White Stripes, At The Drive-in, Mogwai, Franz Ferdinand, Editors, Interpol, Elbow, The Hives, Triggerfinger, Nothing But Thieves, Dua Lipa eva
